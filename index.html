Fat Bear Week

Chunk

1,200 lbs

Chunk is consistently one of the largest and most dominant bears at the falls. He frequently occupies the best fishing spots on the river and has access to the best mating opportunities.

Grazer

1,100 lbs

Grazer is one of the larger and more dominant females to fish the falls. She can be an especially defensive and assertive bear when she has cubs, or when defending a prime fishing location.

Walker

1,250 lbs

Walker is a frequent user of Brooks Falls. He is one of the largest bears to use the river. His size and assertive disposition give him his choice of preferred fishing areas.

Pasghetti

1,200 lbs

Pasghetti preferred fishing spot is at the base of the falls, sandwiched between the lip and the jacuzzi. He has shown great ingenuity in finding this unique spot not frequented by other bears.

Gorge

1,370 lbs

Gorge was emancipated by her mother Holly, this spring. Subadults, essentially the teenagers of the bear world, rank lowest in the bear hierarchy.

Holly

1,150 lbs

Holly was the 2019 Fat Bear Week champion. She has a wide range of fishing spots and is defensive in protecting them, especially when she has cubs.
